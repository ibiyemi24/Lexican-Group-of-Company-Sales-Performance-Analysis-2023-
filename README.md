Lexican Group of Company: Sales Performance Analysis (2023)

Project Overview

This project presents an exploratory analysis of the Lexican Group’s 2023 sales performance using interactive data visualization techniques. The objective was to evaluate key performance indicators across regions, products, customers, sales channels, payment methods, and sales representatives. By transforming raw sales data into an intuitive dashboard, the project supports data-driven decision-making aimed at improving sales efficiency, customer engagement, and overall business performance within the retail industry.

Key Discoveries

•	The Northern region emerged as the highest-performing region, while the Southern region recorded the lowest sales.
•	Clothing was the top-performing product category, whereas Food generated the lowest sales.
•	Returning customers contributed slightly more total sales than new customers, emphasizing retention value.
•	Credit card payments accounted for the highest sales amount, indicating a strong preference for cashless transactions.
•	Retail outlets outperformed online channels in transaction count, despite expectations of digital dominance.
•	David was the top-performing sales representative, while Charlie recorded the lowest sales.
•	Sales showed clear seasonality, with January and October as peak months and September as the weakest.

Identified Problems

•	Noticeable regional performance imbalance, with underperforming regions requiring strategic intervention.
•	Limited differentiation in discount strategies between new and returning customers, reducing loyalty impact.
•	Underutilization of online sales channels, despite growth potential.
•	Performance gaps among sales representatives, indicating uneven skill levels.
•	Absence of profitability and customer demographic data limits deeper financial and behavioral insights.

Methodology

•	Data Source: Public Kaggle retail sales dataset (14 columns, 14,014 records).
•	Tools Used: Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Filters, Slicers).
•	Data Processing: Data cleaning, validation, and restructuring into standard Excel tables.
•	Analysis Techniques: Descriptive analysis, trend analysis, performance comparison, and visual storytelling using bar, line, column, and pie charts.
•	Scope: Sales amount, quantity sold, region, product category, customer type, sales channel, payment method, and sales representative performance.

Insights & Recommendations

•	Regional Strategy: Strengthen marketing and customer engagement in underperforming regions while sustaining momentum in top regions.
•	Product Optimization: Prioritize inventory and promotions for the Clothing category; introduce bundles or discounts for lower-performing products.
•	Customer Strategy: Implement tiered loyalty programs for returning customers and targeted acquisition campaigns for new customers.
•	Sales Channel Strategy: Enhance digital marketing and exclusive online promotions while maintaining strong retail customer experiences.
•	Payment Optimization: Incentivize preferred payment methods with cashback or rewards to increase transaction value.
•	Sales Team Development: Reward high performers and provide training and mentorship for lower-performing sales representatives.
•	Seasonal Planning: Replicate successful strategies from peak months across low-performing periods.

Impact

This project demonstrates the practical value of dashboards in converting complex sales data into actionable business intelligence. The insights enable stakeholders including executives, sales managers, and marketing teams to optimize resource allocation, improve customer retention, enhance sales productivity, and plan strategically for seasonal demand. Beyond Lexican Group, the analytical approach and dashboard framework can be adopted across the retail industry to strengthen data-driven decision-making and sustain long-term business growth.
